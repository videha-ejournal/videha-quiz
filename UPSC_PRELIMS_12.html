The Indian Army's 33 Corps, also known as the Trishul Corps, is headquartered at Sukna near Siliguri in West Bengal, not in Srinagar. 3 Corps is headquartered in Dimapur, Nagaland, and is responsible for military operations in the northeastern region. therefore pair 1 is correctly matched.
